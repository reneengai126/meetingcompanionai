# Meeting Companion AI 
Meeting Companion AI: Facilitating Business Decisions, where Every Meeting Achieves Great Impact (Project for Hackathon)

Meetings go in circles because nobody has named the decision, the options, or what "good enough" looks like before they walk in. Every decision meeting wastes its first 20 minutes because nobody pre-agreed on: what exactly is being decided, what the options are, what a good outcome looks like, and who has a vote vs just an opinion. This agent takes a one-sentence meeting topic ("Should we expand to the EU next quarter?") and generates a structured Decision Brief in under 30 seconds: options with pros/cons, a recommended framing, the key unknowns that would change the answer, and a pre-filled poll sent to Teams before the meeting even starts. The meeting becomes a 15-minute ratification, not a 60-minute debate.
